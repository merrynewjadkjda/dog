How Dogs Breathe

The Unidirectional Flow: Air is split into smelling and breathing paths upon inhalation.

Exhaling for Efficiency: Slits in the side of the nose create air swirls to pull in more scent molecules.

Thermal Regulation: Panting evaporates moisture from the tongue to cool the blood rapidly.
